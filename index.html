About KNOW-HOW-GLOBAL.ORG

The Association Know-How Global e.V. based in Dresden-Germany was founded on 20.10.2017. In a notice dated 5.03.2018, the Income tax department - Dresden South, established that the statutes of the association meets the requirements for tax relief. The association is therefore entitled to issue donation receipts for donations received from members of the public/legal persons and membership fees received from its members. The prerequisite is that the association fulfils the following charitable purposes as provided for in the German Constitution, in the section "Tax-privileged purposes" of the Tax Code: (1). Ecclesiastical purposes (2). Promotion of international cooperation, tolerance in all areas of culture and the ideals of international understanding.
